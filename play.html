How to Play Sudogu

  • Fill the board by placing different dogs in every 3x3 box, row, and column.
  • Choose a dog then tap an empty cell to place it. (You can also tap a cell first, then choose a dog.)
  • To remove an incorrectly placed dog, tap the placed dog once.
  • Open Notes to mark possible dogs for a cell.
  • Use the Erase button to clear notes.

Have fun!
