Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?

If you encounter any issues while playing Sudogu, please send us an email at contact@sudogu.net with a description of the problem, your device type, and operating system version. Screenshots are helpful if possible!

How do I request a feature?

We love hearing your ideas! Send us an email with your feature suggestion and we'll consider it for future updates.

How do I delete my leaderboard data?

To request deletion of your leaderboard data, please email us at contact@sudogu.net with your username and we'll remove your data within 48 hours.

Is Sudogu free to play?

Yes! Sudogu is free to download and play. Enjoy unlimited puzzles without any hidden costs.

What devices are supported?

Sudogu is available for iOS devices (iPhone and iPad) and Android devices. Check the App Store or Google Play for specific compatibility requirements.

Do I need an account to play?

No account is required to play Sudogu. You can enjoy the game without signing up. Creating a username is only needed if you choose to participate in the leaderboard.

Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. Thank you for your patience!
