About Sudogu

The story behind Sudogu

What is Sudogu?

Sudogu is a cozy twist on classic Sudoku. Instead of numbers, you solve puzzles using adorable dogs, keeping the same logic you love, but with a calmer, more playful feel.

Our Mission

We believe puzzle games should be both thoughtful and enjoyable. Sudogu was created by an indie developer inspired by a love for puzzles, a loyal dog, and a time of new beginnings...

Our goal is to offer a calm, engaging experience that keeps the mind active, whether you're a longtime Sudoku fan or just getting started.
